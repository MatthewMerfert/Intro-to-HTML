Dragon Facts Continued

Dragons are really interesting mythical creatures.

Dragon Habitats

Dragons can live basically anywhere. Some live in forests and jungles. Others can live in lakes or oceans. Some can live in the desert or underground. Some live in the skys. There are even some that live in areas that can be considered unhabitable such as in volcanoes.

Different Dragon Forms

Dragons have many different forms. Some have wings while some lack them. Some live in the water while others can't. Some have scales while others can have feathers or even be made of bone. Some can live underground while others are forced to live above ground. Some can even be blind while most can see.
